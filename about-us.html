About Us

Promoting Chess Excellence Across Haryana Since 1995

Our Story

The Haryana Chess Association (HCA) was established in 1995 with a vision to promote chess excellence and develop strategic thinking among the youth of Haryana. Over the past three decades, we have successfully organized hundreds of tournaments, trained thousands of players, and created a vibrant chess community across the state.

Our association works tirelessly to identify and nurture chess talent, provide quality training programs, and create opportunities for players to compete at national and international levels. We believe that chess is not just a game, but a powerful tool for developing critical thinking, concentration, and decision-making skills.
