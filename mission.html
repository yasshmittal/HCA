Our Mission & Vision

Promoting chess excellence and education across Haryana

Our Mission

To promote and develop chess as a sport and educational tool across Haryana, fostering strategic thinking, concentration, and sportsmanship among players of all ages and skill levels. We strive to create opportunities for chess enthusiasts to learn, compete, and excel in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To establish Haryana as a leading chess hub in India, where every individual has access to quality chess education and competitive opportunities. We envision a future where chess becomes an integral part of educational curriculum and community development, producing world-class players and fostering a culture of intellectual growth and strategic excellence.

Our Objectives

Tournament Excellence

Organize high-quality tournaments at district, state, and national levels to provide competitive opportunities for players of all skill levels.

Education & Training

Develop comprehensive chess education programs for schools, colleges, and community centers to promote strategic thinking and cognitive development.

Community Development

Build a strong chess community across Haryana by establishing chess clubs, organizing events, and fostering collaboration among players.

Talent Development

Identify and nurture young chess talents through specialized training programs, coaching camps, and mentorship initiatives.

Partnerships

Collaborate with educational institutions, government bodies, and corporate partners to expand chess reach and resources.

National Recognition

Position Haryana as a leading chess state in India by producing national and international level players and hosting prestigious events.

Our Core Values

Passion

We are driven by our passion for chess and commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Fair Play

We promote integrity, honesty, and fair play in all our tournaments and activities.

Inclusivity

We welcome players of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels, creating an inclusive chess community.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to enhance chess education and tournament experiences.

Our Journey

1995

Foundation

Haryana Chess Association was established with the vision of promoting chess across the state.

2000

First State Championship

Organized the inaugural Haryana State Chess Championship, marking a milestone in chess development.

2010

Education Initiative

Launched chess education programs in government schools across Haryana districts.

2015

National Recognition

Haryana players achieved significant success in national tournaments and championships.

2020

Digital Transformation

Embraced technology with online tournaments and digital chess platforms.

2024

Future Forward

Continuing to expand chess reach and develop world-class players for Haryana.
